# Information

## Welcome to the Team

Welcome to the Haunt Human Resources team! Congratulations on your acceptance. This handbook will outline your duties in your respective position. As a member of our HR team, you are expected to set a good example for the staff team. This means following all of our policies and rules, as well as being active. Activity is especially important from the HR team, as the HR team needs to know the people they manage in order for the team to be as effective as possible in its operations. Activity from HR also encourages the staff team to be active, which improves staff morale and helps their scores!

Each week, you will be expected to meet certain requirements. These requirements vary by position, but certain requirements must be fulfilled by all members of the HR team.

## General Tasks

Those general requirements\* are as follows:

- ​60 messages per week in the staff server
- ​Complete all tasks assigned to you

  <Accordion title="Behavior Manager" defaultOpen icon="shield-check">
    **Overview**

    This is a vitally important and quite complex position, so other, more specific guides are pinned in your channels in the HR category. However, the general outline of your job is to enforce haunt’s staff policies and analyze the performance of the staff team.

    **Tasks**

    Your weekly requirements (in addition to the general ones) are as follows:

    - 25 messages per week in the main server
    - Handle (approve/deny) break requests
    - ​Identify and handle policy violations (both behavioral and activity)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Experience Manager" defaultOpen icon="heart">
    **Overview**

    As the staff experience manager (SEM), it is your job to be there for the staff team and keep morale and activity up. There are many ways of keeping staff morale and activity up, and it is your job to come up with those ideas. As for being there for the staff team, staff members are encouraged to come to you if they have any problems - with the server, another staff member, or a member of management - or if they are having personal problems and would like someone to talk to.

    **Tasks**

    Your weekly requirements (in addition to the general ones) are as follows:

    - ​Send at least another 50 messages per week in the staff server (totaling at least 100)
    - ​Send at least 25 messages per week in the main server
    - ​Come up with ideas to improve staff bonding and team spirit
    - ​Come up with ideas to improve staff activity
    - ​Come up with ideas to increase staff wellbeing and prevent staff burnout
    - ​Host staff-wide biweekly events or carry out one of your ideas each week
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Training Manager" defaultOpen icon="graduation-cap">
    **Overview**

    As the training manager, it is your job to handle staff training. This will mainly be Modmail training, but some departments may have departmental training that you are to help out with and direct. The process of Modmail training will be largely up to you as the Training Manager, but approval from a manager is necessary for large changes (policy changes are also up to you but may be overridden if a manager deems it necessary).

    **​Training Example**

    A general process for Modmail training is as follows: staff member requests training → check if they are eligible → send them the ModMail guide and policies → give them a short quiz on those policies → score them → if they did well, complete an example thread with them → give them their ModMail license → done.

    **Tasks**

    Your weekly requirements (in addition to the general ones) are as follows:

    - ​Maintain the Modmail policies & Modmail guide documents (update them as necessary)
    - ​Train those requesting Modmail training (or other training)
    - ​Enforce the Modmail policies when necessary
    - ​Score the Modmail trainers (if there are any)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
