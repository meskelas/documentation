# Information

## Welcome

We are excited that you have decided to join us and look forward to you being on our team. Our server is primarily about providing support and announcing updates about Haunt. We also focus on community engagement through giveaways and chatting with the members.

The purpose of this handbook is to introduce you to our policies and procedures regarding our expectations for your behavior. If you have any questions about the content, please do not hesitate to contact a manager or the owner.

We want our staff members to enjoy their time on the staff team. This means that we will go through efforts to ensure your well-being isn’t compromised. We believe that personal development is important for our team members, and so we will provide you with resources that you can use to further develop your knowledge in the interests you share with managers and human resources manager.

​If you have any suggestions, feel free to DM Donna to provide your valuable feedback!
