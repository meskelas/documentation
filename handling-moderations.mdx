# Information 

## Documenting Punishments

Punishments should be issued within the staff commands channel. Once it is issued, you must log it with the case create command. An example of the command will be below.

`;case create <user> Reason: <rule> | Evidence: <proof>`

## Punishment Chart

If you don't know what type of punishment to give a user, follow the chart below. 

| Rule | 1st Offence | 2nt Offence | 3rd Offence | 4th Offence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Be Respectful** | Verbal Warning | Bot Warning | 12 - 24h Mute | Ban |
| **Disruption / Spamming** | Verbal Warning | Bot Warning | 6 - 12h Mute | Ban |
| **Harassment** | Bot Warning | 6 - 12h Mute | 12 - 24h Mute | Ban |
| **Bypassing Automod** | Bot Warning | 6 - 12h Mute | 12 - 24h Mute | Ban |
| Advertising | Del \+ Bot Warning | 12 - 24h Mute | 3 - 5d Mute | Ban |
| NSFW Content | Del \+ Bot Warning | 12 - 24h Mute | 3 - 5d Mute | Ban |
| **Common Sense Violations** | Verbal Warning | Bot Warning | 6 - 12h Mute | Ban |
