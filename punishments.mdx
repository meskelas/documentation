# Information

## Issuing Punishments 

When issuing staff infractions, always use the `/infraction-issue`command through Hanna.

Staff actions must be consistent, documented, and based on clear judgment.

## Available Actions 

Staff can receive six types of disciplinary actions.

**Activity Notice** Light reminder about activity or performance.

**Verbal Warning**: Light warning for minor issues.

**Warning**: Formal warning for minor issues.

**Strike**: Serious infraction for repeated behavior.

**Demotion**: Serious infraction for major issues.

**Termination**: Serious infraction for major issues. 

## Choosing Action 

Different rules require different levels of discipline. We trust your judgment, but you must make a clear and confident decision based on the following:

- Severity of the rule broken 
- Intent, either accidental or purposeful
- Impact on our members or the staff
- History of previous infractions
- Risk to safety or privacy

### General Guidelines 

**Minor issues**: Activity notice or verbal warning

**Behavioral issues**: Warning or demotion

**Repeated** issues: Strike or demotion

**Major misconduct**: Demotion or suspension

**Server misconduct**: Terminations and or blacklist
