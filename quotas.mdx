# Information

## Weekly Quota

Every Friday at 5 PM Pacific Daylight Time, the staff team's weekly checks are posted in the [notices channel](https://discord.com/channels/1453096319408144699/1497470609083600927) within the staff server. Each staff member is evaluated on the messages they have sent in both the staff server and the main server, as well as how many times they have bumped the server. 

Staff members are required to engage with the community on a regular basis. Each staff role has a different set of tasks they must follow to keep their position within the team.

## Tasks & Info 

If you know you are unable to meet a weekly check, please request an loa via the [commands channel](https://discord.com/channels/1453096319408144699/1453096320809304212) in the staff server using the `/loa-request` command.

Failure to meet the weekly checks will result in action.
