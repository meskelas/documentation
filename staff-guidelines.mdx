# Information

## Framework

Our guidelines apply to all staff members and provide the framework of principles for conducting daily operations, dealing with other staff, partners, and public members.

All staff members are responsible for their actions. We expect you to handle situations appropriately and use your own judgement.

## Guidelines

<Steps>
  <Step title="Terms Compliance">
    Follow the Discord Terms of Service and any applicable Haunt policies.
  </Step>
  <Step title="User Respect">
    Respect all users. Do not discriminate or harass any user. This includes but is not limited to discrimination due to race, gender identity, sexual orientation, religion, or LGBTQIA\+ identity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Professional Conduct ">
    Exercise professionalism, fairness, equality, and sensitivity in dealing with other staff members, partners, and public members.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Permission Integrity ">
    Do not abuse your permissions. You are entrusted with very powerful permissions. Use of these permissions to complete duties outside your own (with the exception of in cases of nuking/raids or other extenuating circumstances) is prohibited. Outside of extenuating circumstances, your permissions are only to be used to complete your assigned duties. Abuse of permissions to hurt the server or others will result in immediate termination.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Account Standards">
    Keep your account professional. An unprofessional/inappropriate name, status, about section, profile picture, etc., will not be tolerated. If you are asked by an administrator or member of management to change it, change it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Limit Staff Drama">
    Fighting with/arguing with/disrespecting/talking badly about other staff members is prohibited. Handle any types of situations related to this in private channels.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Don't Speak for Haunt">
    Do not speak/make decisions on behalf of Haunt unless given explicit permission by a manager or an owner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="No Bias or Favoritism">
    Making decisions based on previous relationships or interactions is prohibited (moderators and administrators are allowed to take action based on previous behavior, but not because they don’t like someone). Making decisions based on favorite/least favorite server member, staff member, etcetera, is not permitted under any circumstances.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Communication Access">
    Staff members are expected to maintain open methods of communication with all members of Management and Human Resources. Blocking, muting, or ignoring DMs, pings, or other messages from these individuals, whether intentionally or through settings that prevent receipt of communication, is not permitted
  </Step>
  <Step title="Profile Restrictions">
    Your About Me, custom pronouns, and Discord Status may not include anything sexually inappropriate, discriminatory, or  sensitive (does not apply to ‘About Me’ bio)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Medical Leave Review">
    Breaks for medical reasons will be decided on a case-by-case basis. In certain situations, you may be forced to resign if we feel you are unfit to perform the tasks of your position for a period of time greater than what we feel is acceptable.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Channel Privacy ">
    Staff channels are private. Sharing content from these channels will result in immediate action.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Documentation Privacy">
    Staff documentation is private. Sharing content from within any docs will result in immediate action.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Command Usage">
    Commands such as `;kick`, `;ban`, `;warn` & `;case create` in the staff #commands channel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ping Restrictions">
    Mentioning everyone or roles with no valid reason or without permission from an owner or manager will result in punishment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Punishment Documentation">
    Document actions properly. If an action is taken against a public member, that action requires a reason and cannot be deleted without a reason. Document proofs via the `;case create` command.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Age Requirement">
    To be a member of the Human Resources team, you must be at least 17 years of age (some exceptions may be made).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Discretionary Action">
    Haunt reserves the right to take action against staff members for behavior not listed here. If you believe you have been unfairly treated, contact a manager or owner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Policy Updates">
    Haunt reserves the right to update these and other policies at any time, so check back frequently.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Common Sense">
    Obviously, not every policy and regulation could be listed in one document. Use your common sense.
  </Step>
</Steps>
