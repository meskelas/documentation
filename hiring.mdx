## Sourcing Applicants 

There are several methods you can use to source and recruit applicants. Posting looking for staff ads, looking through looking for work channels, and even reaching out to friends, current staff members, or your fellow leadership members to see if they have anyone to refer.

## Handling Applications

The application process consists of a few steps. You will be sending acceptance messages & invites to the staff servers, as well as rejection notices when an application has been denied. Please read every section of this guide carefully, and make sure to return to this guide whenever you have any uncertainty about a certain applicant.
