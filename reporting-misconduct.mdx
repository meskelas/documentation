# Information

## Harassment & Discrimination

Haunt is committed to providing a workplace free from discrimination, sexual harassment, and bullying. Behavior that constitutes discrimination, sexual harassment, or bullying will not be tolerated and will lead to action being taken, which may include termination.

Haunt provides equal opportunity in employment to people without discrimination based on a personal characteristic protected under state and federal equal opportunity legislation.

#### Notes

Any staff members found to have contravened this policy will be subject to disciplinary action, which may include dismissal as outlined in the complaint procedure below.

Staff members must report any behavior that constitutes sexual harassment, bullying, or discrimination to a member of the HR team.

Staff members will not be victimized or treated unfairly for raising an issue or making a complaint.

## File a Report

If you believe you are being, or have been, discriminated against, sexually harassed, or bullied, you should follow this procedure.

- Tell the offender the behavior is offensive, unwelcome, and against server policy and should stop (only if you feel comfortable enough to approach them directly, open a thread via the resources channel). Keep a record of the incident(s).

Staff members should feel confident that any complaint they make is to be treated as confidentially as possible and will not be disclosed to anyone outside of the management team.

## Possible Outcomes

If, after investigation, a manager finds the complaint is justified, management will discuss with the complainant the appropriate outcomes, which may include:

- ​Disciplinary action to be taken against the perpetrator. (counselling, strike, warning, or dismissal)
- ​Counselling for the complainant.
- ​An apology.
